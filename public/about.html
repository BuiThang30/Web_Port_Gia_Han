ABOUT ME

Hi, this is Han and welcome to hanpriartery! This is my little corner of the Internet, where I keep pieces of words, sentences, images, and projects that I care about, and a whole lot of curiosity.

Over the past few years, I’ve been chasing questions of social equity and dignity in governance through schoolwork, leadership, volunteering, and the people I meet along the way. 

I have sat in the offices of pro-bono legal consultants, taught communication and Maths for the deaf in Phú Nhuận, and dug clay in the sunlit fields of Ninh Thuận. Each of these places leaves me with new pieces of the puzzle I am trying to understand, and hanpriartery is where I bring them together :D

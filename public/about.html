ABOUT ME

Hi, this is Han and welcome to hanpriartery! This is my little corner of the Internet, where I keep fragments of words, images, projects that I care about…and a whole lot of curiosity.

I like to get my hands dirty—from drafting policies to teaching kids to shaping clay—and explore how equity takes shape in everyday life. I’ve done just that in the offices of pro bono in District 1, classrooms for the deaf in Phú Nhuận, and the sunlit fields of Ninh Thuận. Each experience adds a piece to the puzzle I’m still piecing together, and hanpriartery is where those fragments form a living mosaic. ໒(⊙ᴗ⊙)७✎▤
